Our Mission. Reduce waste, Increase food Security, and bring a Community closer Together

CatchAndDeliver had a desiring purpose. Our story started with a group of programmers whos sole mission was to create a fully functioning and robust Web Development product. From that task that group not only saw a Web product but a way to tackle a rising issue in todays population. Food Insecurity! Through their combined knowledge they produced a product that defines the very idea that "surplus food" could serve as the tool in waging the war against Food Insecurity

CatchAndDeliver is a conduit that brings fresh clean produce and prepared food from credible local businesses to their local non profit distribution and food bank locations. Our program assist food insecure communities to have access nutrition that fits their needs.

CatchAndDeliver ask the question, "How do we fight against food insecurity?" The answer is "With You". Come and join our mission by becoming apart of our community reach out program.
